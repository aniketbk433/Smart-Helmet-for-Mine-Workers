# Smart-Helmet-for-Mine-Workers
Mining has always been at the core of industrial development, yet it remains one of the most dangerous workplaces on Earth. Every day, thousands of mine workers step into deep, dark, and unpredictable environments where even a small mistake or unnoticed change in surroundings can turn into a life-threatening situation. Toxic gas leaks, extreme temperatures, poor ventilation, and sudden medical emergencies pose continuous risks — and most of these dangers occur silently, without any early warning.
Understanding this serious reality, we felt the need for a solution that genuinely cares for the people who risk their lives underground. This led us to create the Smart Helmet for Mine Workers — a simple, affordable, and innovative safety device designed using modern IoT technology. Instead of relying only on manual supervision or delayed detection, our smart helmet actively monitors the miner’s environment in real time. It checks for toxic gases, tracks temperature, and even senses sudden movement or falls, ensuring that workers are never alone in an emergency.
Our goal is not just to build a helmet — but to build trust, safety, and hope. With this project, we aim to support the hardworking miners of our nation and contribute towards a safer, smarter, and more technologically empowered India in line with STEM for Viksit Bharat and Atmanirbhar Bharat.
